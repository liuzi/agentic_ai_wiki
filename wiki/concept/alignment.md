# Alignment

## Definition

The problem of ensuring AI systems pursue intended goals and behave according to human values, rather than optimizing for unintended objectives or exhibiting harmful behaviors.

## Disambiguation

**Alignment** in AI safety literature refers to several related but distinct problems:

1. **Outer alignment**: Specifying the right objective function
2. **Inner alignment**: Ensuring the model actually optimizes for the specified objective (vs. mesa-optimization)
3. **Distributional robustness**: Maintaining alignment behavior across different contexts and distributions

Sometimes used more broadly to mean "AI safety" or "beneficial AI."
