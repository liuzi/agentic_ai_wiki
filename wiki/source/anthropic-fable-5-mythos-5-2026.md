**Citation:** Anthropic Team. "Claude Fable 5 and Claude Mythos 5." Anthropic Blog, 2026. [URL](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-fable-5-mythos-5) | [Local copy](../../raw/2026/anthropic/Claude%20Fable%205%20and%20Claude%20Mythos%205.md)

**Claim:** Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 represent a new "Mythos-class" capability tier above Opus, launched with novel classifier-based safeguarding that automatically falls back to weaker models rather than refusing requests, plus graduated safeguard removal through trusted access programs.

**Method:** Dual-model release strategy: Fable 5 with safeguards for general use, Mythos 5 with safeguards lifted for trusted partners. Novel safeguarding includes: (1) AI classifiers detecting cybersecurity, biology/chemistry, and distillation misuse with automatic fallback to Opus 4.8, (2) 30-day data retention for monitoring, (3) extensive red-teaming validation, (4) graduated access through Project Glasswing and biology research programs.

**Results:**
- **Capabilities**: State-of-the-art across nearly all benchmarks, highest scores on FrontierCode (coding), Hebbia Finance (reasoning), AAV design (biology)
- **Autonomous work**: Stripe reports months of engineering compressed to days; Mythos 5 completed autonomous genomics research over a week
- **Scientific breakthroughs**: Novel biological hypotheses preferred 80% over Opus-class models; one hypothesis independently corroborated
- **Safeguard effectiveness**: >95% of Fable sessions no fallback; zero compliance with harmful cyber requests across 30 jailbreak techniques
- **Protein design**: 10x acceleration in drug design; 9/14 protein targets yielded strong candidates

**What's new:**
- **Mythos-class tier**: New capability level above Opus with qualitatively different autonomous and scientific capabilities
- **Classifier-based fallback**: AI safeguards that redirect rather than refuse, maintaining user experience while preventing misuse
- **Graduated safeguard removal**: Same underlying model with different safeguard sets for different user classes
- **Autonomous scientific discovery**: First model to consistently generate novel, testable biological hypotheses and conduct independent research
- **Agentic cybersecurity**: Beyond exploit discovery to full multi-step cyberattack capabilities

**What I'd push on:**
- **Capability ceiling unclear**: How much headroom exists above Mythos-class? No comparison to theoretical limits
- **Safeguard robustness**: UK AISI made "progress towards" universal jailbreaks - how close to breakthrough?
- **False positive rates**: "Less than 5% of sessions" have fallbacks but no breakdown by domain or severity
- **Scientific validation**: Only one biological hypothesis independently corroborated so far; most claims based on internal evaluation
- **Pricing sustainability**: Less than half the cost of Mythos Preview despite higher capabilities suggests either efficiency gains or subsidization

**Touches:** Links to classifier-based safeguarding, autonomous scientific capabilities, defensive acceleration expansion, frontier research breakthroughs, AI alignment assessment, cybersecurity capabilities, biology/protein design applications, graduated access models, jailbreak resistance, and benchmark establishment across coding, finance, and biological domains.
